The Kaiju

The Kaiju (Japanese for "Strange Beast") are a race of amphibious, silicon-based creatures engineered by the Precursors to conquer Earth.

  • Category I: The first and weakest, like Trespasser.
  • Category II: Stronger, like Onibaba.
  • Category III: More powerful, like Knifehead.
  • Category IV: A new, deadlier class, like Otachi and Leatherback.
  • Category V: The most powerful, like Slattern.
